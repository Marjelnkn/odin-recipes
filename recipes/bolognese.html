Bolognese

A meat sauce based spaghetti recipe.

A simple meat based sauce that can have spice added to it if desired, goes incredibly well with any kind of pasta, but classicly is done with spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Lean Ground Beef
  • Garlic
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Spaghetti or any other pasta
  • Hot Sauce(optional)
  1. Fine slice the garlic, and toss it in a pan with olive oil already hot.
  2. Throw the beef on, and spread it over the pan. allow it to cook for around a minute
  3. Add herbs, parsley, oregano, etc, to whatever your liking.
  4. As the meat browns, add tomato sauce and stir.
  5. Add whatever is preferred for taste(I use worcestershire)
  6. let simmer for one hour(preferably covered)
  7. boil water in a pot, add pasta when boiling
  8. throw pasta into the pot, once cooked, kill heat on sauce and water
  9. strain pasta and mix the sauce into portions.
  10. add parmesan cheese and serve
