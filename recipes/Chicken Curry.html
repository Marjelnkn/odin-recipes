Chicken Curry

A spicy chicken dish served with an abundance of sauce on a bed of rice or bread.

  • Crushed tomatoes.
  • Tomato Paste.
  • Tomatoe Sauce(optional)
  • Multiple large raw chicen breasts.
  • Garlic
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Curry powder
  • Tumeric, Cumin, Cayenne pepper, and hot peppers.
  • Hot Sauce(optional)
  1. Fine slice the garlic, and toss it in a pan with olive oil already hot.
  2. Cut the chicken breast into small cubes, roughly an inch x inch.
  3. Brown the chicken, then add crushed toamtoes and tomato sauce until the consistency is soupy.
  4. Add a small amount of curry powder, cumin, and tumeric. Stir the mixture.
  5. Cover and let simmer for roughly an hour.
  6. Add cayenne pepper, and curry powder, cumin, or tumeric, until the taste is to your liking.
  7. Let the sauce simmer until it thickens.
  8. Cook rice or bread, or serve alone. Add hot sauce if desired for an extra kick.
