Cevapi

A meat sausage that traditionally comes from eastern Europe.

These small sausages are a staple of eastern European cuisine. They can be eaten alone or served with bread, buns, or chopped in a dish. Cevapi are very versatile with many types of cuisine. They can be bought as veal, pork, or a mix of the two.

Ingredients

  • Cevapi of your choice
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Seasoning
  • Olive Oil
  • Onions
  • Asparagus(optional)
  • Hot Sauce(optional)
  • Bread/buns of your choice(optional)
  1. Marinade the cevapi if desired, otherwise they can be put on raw, with salt & pepper as a minimum.
  2. Preheat a barbecue to around 350 degrees F.
  3. Place the cevapi lined up on the grill, and rotate them every 2-3 minutes until fully cooked.
  4. Baste with barbecue sauce, if so desired.
  5. Add asperagus, covered in olive oil, in a pan to the top the barbecue.
  6. Place buns on top rack and toast, if so desired
  7. Add chopped unions to a small pan and sear, add to cevapi in a bowl afterwards.
  8. Combine ingredients in a dish and serve with any sauces youd like.
